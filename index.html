Corn Soup

Ingredients:

  • 1 can chicken broth
  • 1 can creamed corn
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 oz pork, chopped
  • salt and pepper

Directions:

  1. Pour broth, corn and pork into pot and boil.
  2. Mix eggs and pour into soup, stirring.
  3. Add salt and pepper to taste.
