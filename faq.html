Frequently Asked Questions

About Secret Recipes

What is Secret Recipes?

Secret Recipes is an app that allows users to upload and save their recipes online. The recipes are not viewable by the public because this app is for people with secret family recipes that they may not want to share with everyone.

Why should I use Secret Recipes?

You should use Secret Recipes because it's a convenient way to store all your recipes on the cloud so you never have to worry about losing them again. You can also search your recipes to instantly find the one you want!

Who's behind Secret Recipes?

You can find out more about the team on our About Us page.

Blog

What do you blog about?

We blog about some of our favorite recipes as well as general kitchen and food-related tips and tricks.

Can I submit an article to the blog?

We have a kitchen professional on staff who writes our blog posts for us so we aren't taking blog submissions, but we appreciate the support!

Can I request a topic for a blog article?

Sure! We always value your input. Feel free to drop us a message with your topic request and we'll let you know if we put up an article about it.

How often does your blog update?

We try to update the blog at least once a week.

Features

What features does Secret Recipes have?

We let you upload and save your recipes to the app and set them to either public or private so you can choose which recipes you want to share and which you don't. There's also a search function so you can find recipes fast.

Can I request a feature?

Sure! We love to get feedback from our users. If there's a feature you're dying to have, please let us know.

What new features are you working on?

We're currently working on setting up a family tree so you can log which family member you got your recipes from.
